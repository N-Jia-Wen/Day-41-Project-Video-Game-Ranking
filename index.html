My Top 3 Most Played/Enjoyed Games Right Now

These are the games I've spent the most time playing or have enjoyed/am enjoying the most. Please don't get mad if whatever game of the year isn't here.

Honkai Star Rail

The game I play the most right now. The story is interesting, and there are fun events to play too. The autoplay feature is also useful to avoid burnout.


Splatoon 3

Fun PvP game to spend your time on. It's very competitive, and the developers spice things up with new seasonal weapons and events.


Pokemon Scarlet & Violet

Despite the less than stellar graphics and framerate, I thoroughly liked the open world trajectory that the Pokemon series is heading towards. I also liked the extra gameplay in the DLC (though it's definitely not worth the price tag).
